https://codingflamingo.com/blog/how-to-code-sign-and-notarize-net-console-app-for-mac


Make .p12 in Keychain Manager on Andrew Cummings account
Password for .p12: 1MonKee!!

make base64 with :

base64 -i Certificates.p12 -o Certificates.base64




Sign .app with:

codesign --deep --force --verify --verbose --sign "Your Code Signing Identity" /Users/aricummings/RiderProjects/PointsSlip/bin/Release/net9.0/PointsSlip_x64.app

Remove signature with:

codesign --remove-signature /Users/aricummings/RiderProjects/PointsSlip/bin/Release/net9.0/PointsSlip_x64.app

verify signature with:

codesign -vvv --deep --strict --verbose /Users/aricummings/RiderProjects/PointsSlip/bin/Release/net9.0/PointsSlip_x64.app
